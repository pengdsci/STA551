14. Guidelines for Preparing HTML Presentation Slides

Basic Requirements Suggestions for Effective Presentation
Choose the best of the three projects you completed this semester and create a presentation with 15-20 slides (excluding the title, agenda, and transitional slides) in HTML format.
  • Required Slides:
    • Title and Agenda (not included in the slide limit)
    • Introduction
    • Practical Questions to Address
    • Candidate Models and Justifications
    • Model Identification Process
    • Results
    • Conclusions
    • Limitations.
    Each of the above topics could have multiple slides.
  • Presentation Evaluation Rubric [URL]
  • Visual Aids: Incorporate clean, professional tables and understandable figures. Ensure they meet the following standards:
    • Descriptive Title/subtitle
    • Illustrative Captions
    • Relevant Annotations
    • For figures: Descriptive Labels and a Legend
  • Tips for Presentation Design: Use keywords and phrases instead of full sentences. Focus on telling your story — don’t make your audience read the slides.
